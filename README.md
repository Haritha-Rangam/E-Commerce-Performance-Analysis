# E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard creation using Power BI)
## Project Objective
To analyze e-commerce sales data and evaluate overall business performance including sales, profit, product trends, customer behavior, shipping performance, and regional insights to support strategic decision making.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/Haritha-Rangam/E-Commerce-Performance-Analysis/blob/main/global_superstore%20Assignment%20-%202.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions (KPIs)
- What is the total sales, total profit, and total quantity generated?
- How do weekday and weekend sales and profit compare?
- What is the overall profit margin and margin difference?
- What is the Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and YTD performance?
- Which segment contributes the highest share of revenue?
- Which shipping method generates maximum sales and profit?
- Which are the top 3 revenue-generating products?
- Which are the top 6 profit-generating and bottom 6 profit-draining sub-categories?
- How does revenue contribution vary by sub-category?
- Which customers generate the highest revenue and orders?
- Which region has the highest returned orders?
-What are Total YTD, Total MTD, Total QTD?

-Report URL <a href="https://github.com/Haritha-Rangam/E-Commerce-Performance-Analysis/blob/main/project%20report%20.pbix">View Dashboard</a>

## Process
- Cleaned and transformed the dataset using Power Query.
- Created KPI measures using DAX for sales, profit, quantity, and growth analysis.
- Built interactive dashboards with filters and drill-down functionality.
- Implemented time intelligence calculations including YTD, MTD, and YoY performance analysis.
- Analyzed product, regional, and customer performance to derive business insights.
